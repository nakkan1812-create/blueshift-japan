Privacy Policy

Blueshift Japan LLC · Last updated: June 2026

Blueshift Japan LLC ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect only what we need to respond to you and deliver our service — nothing more.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you provide via our brief form or by email: your name, company, work email, website, and the details of your Japan market question (product, target customer, goals).
  • Engagement information you share so we can prepare your report. This may include confidential business information.
  • We do not intentionally collect special-category personal data, and we ask that you not send payment card numbers by email.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and send a proposal.
  • To research, prepare, and deliver your market entry report and any agreed follow-up.
  • To send service-related communications about your engagement.

3. Confidentiality & AI

Your information is treated as strictly confidential. We do not sell or rent your data, and we do not share it with third parties except the limited service providers below. We are glad to sign your NDA before you share sensitive material.

How we use AI. We use AI tools to speed up research on publicly available market data. Any confidential details you share are handled only in enterprise or commercial API environments — never consumer AI products — under terms where your inputs are not used to train models and are subject to zero-data-retention processing (inputs are not stored after the request is served). In short: AI helps us research the market faster — it is never trained on, and never retained from, what you tell us in confidence.

You control what the AI ever sees. Our market analysis runs on public sources; your confidential materials are not required to produce most reports. Where you do share unreleased product specs or sensitive plans, we are glad to sign your NDA first, and we can work from anonymized or redacted inputs so that identifying details never leave your side.

4. Service providers

We use a small number of trusted providers to operate the business, who process data only on our behalf:

  • Formspree — processes submissions from our brief form.
  • Google (Gmail / Workspace) — email correspondence.
  • Stripe — payment processing (we do not store your card details).

Some providers may process data outside Japan; we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protection.

5. Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal and accounting obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymized. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent to marketing at any time. Contact us at the address below and we will respond without undue delay.

7. Cookies & analytics

This site is intentionally lightweight. We do not use advertising cookies. If we add basic analytics in future, we will update this policy.

8. Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of Japan, and is handled in line with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI).

9. Contact

Questions or requests: ceo@blueshift-japan.com
